11. TETRA Vault Architecture

The TETRA Vault is the logical data isolation model underlying the TETRA platform. These are architectural facts, not marketing commitments:

  • Logical isolation: Each organization's data is logically isolated from every other organization's data at the storage, query, and API levels. Your data does not share a storage context with any other customer.
  • No cross-customer pooling: Customer Data is never combined with, compared against, or aggregated with the data of any other customer for any purpose — analytical, commercial, or operational.
  • Customer-controlled permissions: Access permissions to your TETRA Vault are set by you. InPursuit Health personnel access your Vault only for support purposes explicitly requested by you, and only with your authorization recorded in the audit log.
  • Immutable audit logs: All access to Customer Data generates an immutable, timestamped audit record. These logs cannot be altered or deleted — by you or by InPursuit Health — during the applicable retention period. You have real-time access to your full audit log through the platform.
  • TETRA Aegis™ enforcement: All AI model interactions with data in your Vault are mediated by TETRA Aegis™ before any data leaves your organization. TETRA Sentinel™ monitors continuously for anomalies, policy violations, and unauthorized access attempts.

12. AI Governance

12.1 The TETRA Conductor™ Principle

InPursuit Health does not pick AI model winners. TETRA Conductor™ is model-agnostic infrastructure. Any AI model — commercial or open source — can operate within the TETRA orchestration layer. We are the rails, not the train. This means your organization is never locked into a single AI vendor and can migrate models without changing your data infrastructure.

12.2 TETRA Aegis™ — Mandatory Supervisory Control

Every AI interaction within the TETRA enterprise platform passes through TETRA Aegis™. This is an architectural constraint, not a policy setting. No AI model — regardless of vendor — interacts with PHI or clinical data without passing through the TETRA Aegis supervisory layer. This layer intercepts, classifies, risk-scores, and mediates every interaction in under 200ms. You cannot opt out of this protection.

12.3 No AI Black Boxes

Every AI-generated recommendation, risk score, and care gap alert produced through the TETRA platform includes the full reasoning chain. Explainability is not optional and cannot be disabled. All AI decisions are logged in your immutable audit trail.

12.4 Bias Monitoring

We continuously test AI models operating within the TETRA platform for demographic bias in risk scoring, care recommendations, and patient outreach prioritization. Equitable care delivery is an engineering requirement, not a policy statement.

12.5 What We Will Never Do With AI

  • Sell AI-derived insights built on your patient population to any third party
  • Share anonymized data sets, model weights, or predictive models trained on your data with any other organization
  • Allow third-party AI vendors to retain, train on, or analyze your clinical data without an explicit, described, written authorization from you
  • Deploy autonomous clinical AI that makes patient care decisions without a licensed provider in the loop
