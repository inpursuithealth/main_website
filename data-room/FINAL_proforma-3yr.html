Key Assumptions
What Drives These Numbers.
VBC / ACCESS Model: $47 blended PMPM (conservative vs. CMS estimates). 35% blended InPursuit share (weighted toward Tier 1 Full Service). 70% chronic condition prevalence among Medicare beneficiaries. 6-week deployment per provider. $60K CAC. $96K annual cost to serve per provider. OAPs begin July 2026 (Cohort 1) and mid-2027 (Cohort 2).

D2C: $22 blended monthly ARPU across all paid tiers. 2% free-to-paid conversion in Year 1, scaling to 6.7% by Year 3 as features mature and trust builds. Commerce revenue begins Year 2 at modest levels ($0.40 per free user per year) and scales with user base and advertiser demand.

Telehealth: $85 blended revenue per encounter across urgent care, CCM, weight management, mental health. Volume grows as provider and D2C user bases expand. No external marketing spend assumed — encounters are organic from existing user base.

Costs: Headcount scales from ~15 (SAFE phase) to ~55 (100 providers at scale). Cloud infrastructure costs scale linearly with patient volume. No federal contract revenue in base case. Working capital buffer included for OAP timing lag.
