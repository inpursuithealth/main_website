Instrument Simple Agreement for Future Equity (SAFE) — post-money valuation cap, no discount

Discount Rate None — valuation cap provides early investor advantage
Execution Electronically via Carta
Investor Qualification Accredited investors only (self-certification at intake; Carta verification at execution)
Conversion Mechanics
Conversion Trigger Next priced equity financing round (the "Equity Financing")
Planned Equity Financing Series A Preferred — $20,000,000
Target close: September 1, 2026
Conversion Price The lower of: (a) the price per share in the Equity Financing, or (b) the price implied by the $25,000,000 valuation cap
Resulting Securities Same class and rights as shares issued in the Equity Financing (Series A Preferred or equivalent)
Pro Rata Rights Per standard SAFE terms
Most Favored Nation If the Company issues subsequent SAFEs on more favorable terms prior to conversion, this SAFE will be amended to match
Additional Terms
Liquidity Event In the event of a Change of Control or IPO prior to conversion, the investor receives the greater of: (a) the SAFE investment amount, or (b) the amount payable based on conversion at the valuation cap
Dissolution Event Investor receives repayment of SAFE investment amount, junior to outstanding indebtedness but senior to common equity
Information Rights Quarterly summary updates on financial performance and material developments
Governing Law State of Wyoming
SAFE Form Based on the standard Y Combinator post-money SAFE template, with modifications as reflected in the final Carta-executed document
Use of Proceeds
40% — Product CMS MVP demonstration (March 31, 2026) across all three patient-facing categories in the Medicare App Library
25% — Activation TETRA™ deployment at Honeycomb Medical Group; patient enrollment; first billable encounters
20% — Pipeline ACCESS Model provider outreach; MNDA and LOI pipeline for Cohort 1 and Cohort 2 targets
15% — Operations NPI registration, telehealth, legal, compliance, Carta SAFE execution, data room
SAFE Investor Advantage. Investors in the SAFE receive a $25,000,000 valuation cap. If the Series A prices above this cap, SAFE holders convert at the cap price — receiving more equity per dollar invested than Series A participants. The earlier the risk taken, the greater the reward at conversion.
