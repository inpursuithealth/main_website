Executive Summary
The Gap Between Monitoring and Meaningful Action
The Problem

Traditional RPM requires patients to wear devices, charge sensors, and manually transmit data — creating 64% non-adherence rates in Medicare populations. Without continuous, frictionless monitoring, chronic disease management remains reactive rather than preventive.

CMS spends over $380 billion annually on chronic disease management. A significant portion is attributable to conditions that are detectable, manageable, and preventable with consistent biometric surveillance.

The status quo costs Medicare an estimated $47,000 per avoidable hospitalization — events that continuous RPM is clinically shown to prevent.

The Solution

InPursuit Reflect™ leverages rPPG (remote photoplethysmography) to extract 8+ validated vital signs from a 30-second smartphone camera scan — no wearable, no hardware, no friction. The data flows directly into TETRA™, our FHIR-compliant health data orchestration layer.

By integrating with CMS Innovation Models (ACO REACH, MSSP, GUIDE, PCF), Reflect™ enables providers to proactively identify at-risk patients, reduce hospitalizations, and qualify for enhanced VBC incentive payments.

Reflect™ is not another RPM device. It is a population health infrastructure layer designed from the ground up for value-based care compliance and CMS alignment.
