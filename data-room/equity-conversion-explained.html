How It Works
Own from Day 1. Convert at Series A.
Unlike SAFEs or convertible notes, a priced equity investment gives you real ownership immediately. You receive LLC membership units on close, participate in tax benefits right away, and convert to preferred stock when InPursuit becomes a C-Corp.
Step 1 — Today

Immediate Ownership

Your $100K at a $25M pre-money valuation gives you 0.40% ownership from Day 1. Units are issued and reflected in the operating agreement.

$100K ÷ $25M = 0.40% ownership
  • LLC membership units issued at close
  • Reflected in operating agreement and cap table
  • K-1 tax reporting begins immediately
  • Pro-rata rights and information rights from Day 1
Step 2 — Series A

C-Corp Conversion

When InPursuit converts to a C-Corp at the Series A, your LLC units convert to preferred stock 1:1. This unlocks additional protections on top of benefits already received.

LLC Units → Preferred Stock (1:1)
  • QSBS eligibility (Section 1202) — up to $10M tax-free gains
  • Section 1244 loss protection — ordinary loss treatment
  • Plus R&D credits and QBI deductions already received as LLC member
  • Liquidation preference and anti-dilution protections

Why this matters: Most early-stage investors choose SAFEs or notes, which defer ownership until a priced round. With priced equity, you own from Day 1 and start receiving tax benefits immediately. When the C-Corp conversion happens, you layer on QSBS and Section 1244 — protections that SAFE holders only begin qualifying for at that point.
