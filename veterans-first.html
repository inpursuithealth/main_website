The Problem

For a C3 quadriplegic, voice is the primary — often only — interface to the world. Consumer smart home devices connect to the general internet: music, weather, shopping. But they don't connect to the systems that matter most — VA medical records, prescription management, provider scheduling, and emergency services.

Charlie couldn't refill his own medications. He couldn't check his VA appointment. He couldn't alert his care team without someone physically present to make the call.

The Breakthrough

Using TETRA's interoperability engine, we built LivSolo Connect — a single Alexa skill that bridges consumer voice technology to enterprise healthcare systems. One wake word. Unlimited capabilities.

Charlie says "Alexa, open LivSolo" — and TETRA connects him to VA VistA, pharmacy systems, telehealth scheduling, prescription tracking, package notifications, and even 911 emergency services with automatic caregiver alerts.
