VBC Customer Base vs. Total Provider Market
Bottom Line Up Front (BLUF)

Your VBC customer base (~830K physicians, ~60K facilities in VBC arrangements) is roughly 35–40% of the total US provider market. That's your beachhead — the people who already understand VBC and need interoperability now.

The other 60% (~370K physicians + ~380K facilities) is where the massive growth is. CMS is mandating that all Traditional Medicare beneficiaries be in accountable care by 2030. Those providers will need data interoperability. They just don't know it yet.

Strategy: Win VBC-first customers (they're in pain now), then ride the CMS mandate wave as the market expands to 100%.
