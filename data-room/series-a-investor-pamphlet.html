Why This Requires Infrastructure, Not Another App

Every payer—CMS, UnitedHealthcare, Aetna, Cigna, Blue Cross, state Medicaid programs, self-insured employers—is moving toward paying for outcomes instead of volume. That transition requires a fundamentally new orchestration layer: one that federates fragmented data across every EHR, payer, pharmacy, lab, and wearable device, then makes it actionable at the point of care. The infrastructure must work across all payers simultaneously, not just one.
