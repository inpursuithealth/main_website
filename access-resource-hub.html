Understanding ACCESS

Outcome-Aligned Payments replace
visit-based billing.

ACCESS stands for Accountable Care Coordinated Systems of Specialty Services. It replaces per-visit billing with monthly per-patient payments for managing chronic conditions — whether or not the patient comes in.

Enhanced CKM
Cardiovascular · Kidney · Metabolic
$720 / patient / yr
Full risk cohort — complex cardiovascular, chronic kidney disease stages 3–5, and metabolic conditions.
Standard CKM
Cardiovascular · Kidney · Metabolic
$480 / patient / yr
Moderate-risk cardiovascular and metabolic patients without advanced CKD.
Musculoskeletal
MSK · Orthopedic · Pain
$360 / patient / yr
Chronic MSK conditions — osteoarthritis, chronic pain, post-surgical recovery management.
Behavioral Health
Mental Health · Substance Use
$300 / patient / yr
Depression, anxiety, substance use disorder — integrated behavioral health coordination.
50/50
Withhold Structure
Half paid monthly upfront. Half returned at year-end based on outcomes performance.
~$30
Co-Management Pay
Per specialist review event when co-managing ACCESS patients with PCPs.
0%
Patient Coinsurance
ACCESS waives patient cost-sharing — removing the financial barrier to specialist referrals.
