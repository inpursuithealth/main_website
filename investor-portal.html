Regulation D — Accredited Investor Status

The materials in this data room constitute confidential information pertaining to a private securities offering by InPursuit Health, LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company. Access is restricted to Accredited Investors as defined under Rule 501 of Regulation D under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.

You qualify as an Accredited Investor if you meet one or more of the following:

  • Individual Income: Annual income exceeding $200,000 (or $300,000 jointly with spouse) in each of the two most recent calendar years, with reasonable expectation of the same for the current year.
  • Net Worth: Individual or joint net worth exceeding $1,000,000, excluding your primary residence.
  • Professional Certification: Holder of a valid Series 7, Series 65, or Series 82 license in good standing.
  • Entity: An entity with total assets exceeding $5,000,000, or in which all equity owners are individually accredited investors.
  • Knowledgeable Employee: A knowledgeable employee of a registered investment fund (where applicable).
