The Infrastructure
Five Layers. One Stack. No Competitor Has All Five.

TETRA™

Core orchestration engine. Federates data across EHRs, payers, pharmacies, labs, and wearables. Validated at 41K+ server scale by DoD/VA. Selected over IBM, Oracle, SAP in formal AoA. 135K writes/sec. 1B+ cached records.

TETRA Ex™

Health data exchange. Real-time bidirectional interoperability. Partnerships with Particle Health, b.well Connected Health, and eHealth Exchange. Network effect grows with every provider onboarded.

TETRA Conductor™

Model-agnostic AI orchestration. Evaluates clinical context in real-time and routes to the optimal AI model for each interaction. No vendor lock-in. AI models become interchangeable instruments.

TETRA Aegis™

AI governance control plane. Patent-pending (provisional filed Feb 2026). Enforces validation, risk scoring, and containment. The trust layer between AI systems and healthcare data.

TETRA Sentinel™

Behavioral surveillance. Hallucination detection, drift monitoring, adversarial defense. Structurally independent from the models it supervises — a safety requirement, not a feature.
