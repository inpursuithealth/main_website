Investment Thesis
Infrastructure for Healthcare's Generational Shift.
The federal government is actively dismantling the fragmented, provider-centric healthcare system and rebuilding it around the patient. That transformation requires an orchestration layer to federate data, enable value-based care, power consumer engagement, and govern AI safely. InPursuit built that layer. The federal government already proved it works. CMS selected it for all three patient-facing categories. This SAFE funds the critical bridge from validation to revenue.

The Problem

The current healthcare system was built for billing, not patients. Transitioning 140+ million Medicare beneficiaries to outcome-based care requires real-time data interoperability across every EHR, payer, pharmacy, lab, and wearable device. That infrastructure does not exist today — except inside TETRA™.

The Opportunity

CMS mandates that all Medicare beneficiaries be in accountable, outcome-based care by 2030. The ACCESS Model pays providers monthly per-patient fees for 10 years. There are 300,000 physicians in the U.S., and the vast majority lack the infrastructure to participate. InPursuit is the technology backbone that gets them there.

The SAFE Bridge

$2M deployed across 120 days delivers the CMS MVP demonstration on April 9, activates Honeycomb Medical Group as the first revenue-generating provider, and builds the LOI pipeline for ACCESS Cohort 1 & 2 applications. Every milestone has a deadline and a deliverable.

The Payoff

SAFE investors receive preferential conversion terms when the Series A closes at up to $20M. Early risk is rewarded with a $25M cap on a company positioned at the center of a $5.6 trillion market undergoing its most significant structural transformation since Medicare itself.
