Important Disclosures
Assumptions & Risk Factors
ACCESS OAP Reconciliation: CMS pays 50% of OAPs monthly. The remaining 50% is withheld and reconciled after each 12-month care period. Full payment requires meeting the Outcome Attainment Threshold (50% of patients meeting clinical targets). Revenue projections assume this threshold is met. If not met, the withheld portion is reduced or forfeited.
RPM/CCM Billing: Full direct billing under InPursuit's NPI requires establishment of InPursuit Medical, PC (Phase 2). Year 1 RPM/CCM revenue is limited, reflecting the time required to operationalize the PC entity and MSA with InPursuit Health, LLC.
D2C Growth: User acquisition projections assume Medicare App Library listing (July 2026), app store distribution, and referral from provider network. Actual growth rates may vary. Commerce revenue depends on brand partner adoption of transaction-moment bidding model.
No Revenue Sharing: InPursuit does not charge providers a percentage of VBC revenue. All InPursuit revenue comes from direct payer billing under its own NPI and consumer subscriptions/commerce. Referring providers earn CMS co-management payments independently (~$100/patient/year/track), paid by CMS directly.
Forward-Looking Statements: These projections are management estimates based on current CMS payment rates, market conditions, and operational plans. Actual results may differ materially. This document does not constitute an offer to sell securities.
