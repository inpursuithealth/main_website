Investment Thesis
Capture the Dominant Share of ACCESS Model Providers.
The CMS ACCESS Model pays primary care practices monthly per-patient fees for Medicare beneficiaries with chronic conditions — for 10 years. There are 300,000 physicians in the U.S. The vast majority lack infrastructure to participate. InPursuit deploys TETRA™ at zero upfront cost and shares in the new revenue.

Cohort 2 (January 2027) is the land grab. Series A capital targets 75–100 providers. At $47 avg PMPM and blended 35% InPursuit share, that produces $50–79M ARR by 2028 with 85%+ gross margins and 42:1 LTV:CAC. ACCESS applications continue rolling through 2033.
