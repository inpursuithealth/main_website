How It Works

Five seconds to ownership.

TETRA Vault sits beneath every InPursuit product as the single source of truth for your health data.

01

Connect Your Sources

Link hospitals, labs, pharmacies, payers, wearables, and patient portals. TETRA Ex aggregates your complete health record from every connected source in seconds — not days.

02

AI Assembles Your Health Identity

TETRA Conductor deduplicates, normalizes, and enriches your data into a unified health timeline. Medications, diagnoses, lab trends, wearable data, and AI-generated insights — structured and indexed.

03

Encrypted with Your Keys

The assembled health identity is encrypted with a key pair generated on the consumer's device. InPursuit never sees, stores, or has access to these keys. This is the architectural guarantee: not a policy choice — a structural exclusion.

04

Governed by TETRA Aegis

Every access request, every AI query, every share event passes through TETRA Aegis — the governance layer that enforces your consent preferences and logs every action in an immutable audit trail.

05

Share, Revoke, Export — Anytime

Grant time-bound, purpose-limited access to providers, caregivers, AI apps, or researchers. Revoke any share in real time. Export or delete your entire vault at any time, instantly.
