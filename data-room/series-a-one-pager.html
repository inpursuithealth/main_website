The Problem

The $5.6T U.S. healthcare system was built for billing, not patients. Provider data is siloed across thousands of incompatible EHRs. Every payer—CMS, commercial, MA—is mandating outcome-based care. AI is entering healthcare faster than anyone can govern it. The transition cannot happen without a new orchestration layer. TETRA is that layer.
