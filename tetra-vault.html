Core Architecture

Built on four unbreakable pillars

Every design decision in TETRA Vault flows from a single conviction: your health data is yours alone.

01

Zero-Trust Encryption

AES-256 encryption at rest with per-record key derivation. Data is encrypted before it leaves your device and decrypted only when you authorize access — never on our servers in plaintext.

02

Consent-Driven Access

You define exactly who sees what, for how long, and for what purpose. Every access event — by any AI model, provider, or app — is logged in an immutable, timestamped audit trail only you can view.

03

Interoperable Portability

FHIR R4 and HL7-compliant data structures mean your Vault travels with you. Export to any provider, import from any system, and revoke access instantly — no calls, no forms, no friction.

04

Immutable Audit Trail

Every read, write, and share event is cryptographically signed and stored in an append-only log. You always know exactly who accessed your data, when, and what they did with it.

05

Federated Storage

Your data is sharded and distributed across redundant nodes. No single server holds your complete record. Even in a breach, attackers obtain encrypted fragments that are cryptographically useless without your key.

06

Never Sold. Never.

TETRA Vault is governed by the InPursuit Data Oath. Your health data is not a product. It is not monetized, aggregated for advertising, or shared with insurers. This is a binding legal commitment, not a marketing promise.

How It Works

From ingestion to insight — secured

TETRA Vault sits beneath every InPursuit product as the single source of truth for your health data.

1
Ingestion

Data arrives from EHRs, labs, pharmacies, wearables, and manual uploads. It is validated, normalized to FHIR R4, and encrypted immediately.

2
Encryption

Each record is encrypted with a unique key derived from your master key. Metadata is indexed separately so search works without decrypting records.

3
Permissioning

You grant time-bound, purpose-limited access tokens. TETRA Conductor, AI models, and providers receive only what you approve — nothing more.

4
Audit & Control

Every access event writes to your immutable log. Revoke any token in real time. Export or delete your entire Vault at any time, instantly.
