Revenue Model
Direct Payer Billing. Four Independent Streams.

InPursuit partners with small-to-mid-size physician groups who refer patients with chronic conditions. InPursuit delivers technology-enabled care and bills payers directly under its own NPI for ACCESS OAPs, RPM, CCM, and telehealth. Referring providers earn CMS co-management payments (~$100/patient/year/track) paid by CMS independently. InPursuit never charges a percentage of VBC revenue.
