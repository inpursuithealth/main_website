How It Works

Five seconds to ownership.

A consumer's entire journey from fragmented records to sovereign health identity happens in a single session. No portals to navigate. No forms to fill. No waiting.

01

Consumer Requests Their Records

A single tap initiates the aggregation. TETRA Ex connects to every available exchange — TEFCA QHINs, HIE networks, payer systems, patient portals — and pulls the consumer's complete health record.

02

TETRA Normalizes and Enriches

TETRA's data orchestration layer deduplicates records from multiple sources, resolves conflicting entries, and TETRA Conductor applies AI-driven curation to build a unified, comprehensible health identity — not a stack of clinical documents.

03

Consumer-Only Encryption

The assembled health identity is encrypted with a key pair generated on the consumer's device. InPursuit never sees, stores, or has access to these keys. This is the architectural guarantee: not a policy choice, not a configuration setting — a structural exclusion.

04

The Vault is Delivered

The consumer now holds a complete, encrypted health identity in their TETRA Vault. It includes their unified health record, AI-generated insights, a longitudinal timeline, and a consent ledger that will track every future sharing decision.

05

Share, Revoke, Repeat

From a single control point, the consumer can grant any physician, family member, caregiver, or application access to specific data elements — scoped, time-limited, and revocable. Every action is logged. Every permission is the consumer's to give and take away.
